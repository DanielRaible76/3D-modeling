1930 Aspects:  
Red - Stop  
Red Over Yellow - Proceed at low speed prepared to stop- Track Occupied  
Yellow - Proceed at low speed prepared to stop- Track Unoccupied  
Green - Proceed at low speed- Track Unoccupied, Next signal clear
